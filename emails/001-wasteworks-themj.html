Modernising citizen access to local authority waste services online

Checking bin days, reporting missed bins, paying for bulky or green waste collections - now more than ever, it’s crucial that citizen access to each of these services is easy, inclusive and intuitive.

That’s why SocietyWorks has partnered with Bromley Council to produce WasteWorks, a modern, reliable, citizen-friendly waste service for local authorities.

What does WasteWorks do for councils and for your residents?

  • Improve the user experience - one easy-to-navigate place to access all aspects of waste online, with the ability to self-serve requests for new bins and assisted collections or payments for bulky or green waste subscriptions.
  • Create channel shift - reduce demand on customer service centres and save money with an online reporting system that anyone can use.
  • Streamline your workflow - full integration with in-cab systems that improves how data is shared.
  • Deliver an open and transparent service - manage expectations and handle seasonal demand with templated responses and automatic updates.
  • Prioritise accessibility - a service that works across all devices and which meets WCAG 2.1 as standard.

What motivates citizens to report local problems and how can local government and other public sector organisations provide an up-to-date service that makes the reporting process a more convenient, constructive and successful task? Understand the latest expectations UK citizens have of public sector reporting services in SocietyWorks’ new report, with research carried out in collaboration with YouGov.

SocietyWorks is a subsidiary of civic technology charity mySociety. For over a decade, we’ve been helping councils and other public bodies to build, discover and redesign their digital reporting services in a way that puts citizens at the front and centre. For the first time, we’re sharing some of our research into what citizens want, and don’t want, from a reporting service, to help you stay informed of ever-changing requirements.
